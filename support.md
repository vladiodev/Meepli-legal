## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I add a new item?**  
A: Tap the “Add Item” button and fill in the product details, expiration date, and attach any relevant documents.

**Q: How do I receive notifications for expirations?**  
A: Make sure notifications are enabled for Meepli in your device settings. The app will notify you 3 days before items expire.

**Q: Can I access my data on another device?**  
A: Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. There is no cloud sync available.
